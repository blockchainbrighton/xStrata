## ðŸŽ¯ The Narrative: "Sovereign Data Strata"

**The Problem:**
Layer 1 Bitcoin inscriptions (Ordinals) are revolutionary but expensive and limited in throughput. Other Layer 2 solutions often rely on centralized indexers or ephemeral storage.

**The Solution:**
This protocol offers **"Sovereign Data Strata"** on Stacks. It treats the Stacks blockchain not just as a transaction ledger, but as a verifiable, programmable hard drive for the Bitcoin economy. It allows for:
1.  **Permanence**: Data is etched into the Stacks chain, secured by Bitcoin's hash power.
2.  **Composability**: Unlike static NFTs, these inscriptions can reference each other (Recursion), allowing developers to build "Hyper-Structures" (apps, albums, games) entirely on-chain.
3.  **Utility**: Because they exist in Clarity smart contracts, this data is instantly readable by other contracts (DeFi, DAOs, Licensing).

## ðŸ§  Naming Brainstorm

We need a name that conveys **Structure, Permanence, Layers, and Utility**.

### Category 1: Structural / Geological (The "Layer" Angle)
*Connecting to "Stacks" and "Bitcoin" (digital gold/rock).*

*   **Strata**: Layers of rock or atmosphere. Implies history and permanence.
    *   *Tagline*: "Etch your data into the Strata."
*   **Basalt**: A common volcanic rock. Hard, foundational.
    *   *Tagline*: "The bedrock of the new internet."
*   **Core**: The central part of the earth/computer.
    *   *Tagline*: "Essential on-chain storage."
*   **Geode**: A rock containing a surprise (data) inside.
*   **Monolith**: A large, single upright block of stone.

### Category 2: Functional / Constructive (The "Building" Angle)
*Focusing on the recursive/composable nature.*

*   **Loom**: Weaving data threads together.
    *   *Tagline*: "Weave the decentralized web."
*   **Nexus**: A connection or series of connections linking two or more things.
    *   *Tagline*: "The nexus of Bitcoin data."
*   **Syntax**: The arrangement of words/data.
*   **Codex**: An ancient manuscript in book form.
    *   *Tagline*: "The Codex of Stacks."
*   **Mosaic**: A picture produced by arranging small colored pieces (chunks).

### Category 3: Abstract / Evocative (The "Vibe" Angle)
*Modern, Web3-native, catchy.*

*   **Aion**: Hellenistic deity associated with unbounded time/eternity.
*   **Echo**: (Audio bias) A reflection of sound, but also "persisting data".
*   **Flux**: Continuous change (data flow).
*   **Kachina**: (Spirit/Messenger) - A bit esoteric.
*   **Imprint**: Leaving a mark.
